Sign up for reminders



Instructions

Enter the email address you wish to receive reminders at, the course identifier for the course you wish to receive reminders for, and the date you wish to receive the first email.

Currently, we are only accepting courses from MIT OpenCourseWare - support for other providers will be coming soon.

We prefer that you specify the course using the MIT Course Number, for example, 18.034 for the course 18.034 - Honors Differential Equations. Type this into the Course Identifier field of the form. If you cannot unambiguously identify your course in this manner, just use the url of the OCW page.
